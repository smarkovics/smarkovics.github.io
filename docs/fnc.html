Fruit Ninja Classic+ is a game on Apple Arcade which was a throwback to be more like the original Fruit Ninja.

My Contributions
I joined the project after it had been initally released onto Apple Arcade. The purpose was to increase retention through liveopping and updates.
  • Level Track
  • Missions
  • Leaderboards - Both local and through Game Center
  • Achievements - Both local and through Game Center
  • New Blades and Blade powers
  • fortnightly/ monthly events
For the Game Center functionality I created A plugin that was used to bridge the c# and the Objective-c code. This plugin was also used in rebuilds of Fish Out of Water and Age of Zombies when they were ported to Unity from an internal Engine.
